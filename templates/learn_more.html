💚 Smart Organ Donation Management System

The Smart Organ Donation System is a next-generation platform that digitizes and simplifies the organ donation process — securely connecting donors, recipients, and hospitals with transparency and efficiency.

🌍 Our Mission

We aim to bridge the gap between donors and recipients using technology-driven innovation. The system ensures accurate organ matching, verified communication, and ethical allocation, saving countless lives through digital transformation.

⚙️ Key Features

🔐 Secure Registration

Separate role-based registration for donors, recipients, and hospitals with password encryption.

📱 OTP Verification

Ensures user authenticity through mobile-based OTP verification for account creation and reset.

👥 Role Management

Assign permissions for admins, hospitals, and coordinators with ease.

🩸 Smart Matching Algorithm

Automatically matches donors and recipients by organ type, blood group, and city proximity.

📊 Admin Dashboard

Provides centralized analytics and live tracking of ongoing allocations and registrations.

📩 Real-time Notifications

Instant alerts for organ matches, approvals, and critical updates to keep everyone informed.

🔄 Workflow Overview

  1. User chooses role (Donor, Recipient, or Hospital) and registers securely.
  2. OTP verification confirms authenticity of registration.
  3. Donors list available organs; recipients submit requests.
  4. Hospitals validate details and allocate organs based on compatibility.
  5. Real-time notifications keep all stakeholders updated instantly.
